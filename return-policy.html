Return and Refund Policy

Last Updated: 02/03/2023
Thank you for your business! We at Synapse Fit want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Return and Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which you can return your smartband for a refund or exchange.

Return Window
You have 15 days from the date of purchase to return your smartband.

Eligible Returns
To be eligible for a return and full refund, your smartband must be:
In original, unopened packaging with all manuals, accessories, and charging cables included.
In unused and unworn condition, with no scratches, marks, or damage.
Accompanied by a copy of your receipt or proof of purchase.

Non-Returnable Items
The following items are not eligible for return or refund:
Opened or damaged smartbands.
Smartbands purchased more than 15 days ago.
Items not purchased directly from Synapse Fit (e.g., authorized retailers, online marketplaces).

Reason for Return
We accept returns for the following reasons:
Change of mind: If you simply decide you don't want the smartband anymore, you can return it within the return window.
Defective product: If your smartband is malfunctioning or not working as advertised, please contact us for a replacement or refund.
Incorrect item received: If you receive a different smartband than the one you ordered, please contact us immediately for a replacement.

Return Process
To initiate a return, please follow these steps:
Contact our customer service team at synapsefitshop@gmail.com or +94 77 612 2332 to request a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number.
Pack the smartband securely in its original packaging, along with a copy of your receipt or proof of purchase.
Clearly mark the package "Return - RMA#[We'll Provide it]" and ship it back to the address we provide.

Return Shipping Costs
Change of mind: You will be responsible for the return shipping costs.
Defective product or incorrect item received: We will cover the return shipping costs.

Refunds
Once we receive your returned smartband and verify that it meets the return criteria, we will issue a full refund to the original payment method within 28 business days.

Exchanges
If you wish to exchange your smartband for a different model, please contact our customer service team to check availability and discuss any price differences. We will then provide you with instructions on how to return your original smartband and complete the exchange.

Warranty Claims
For warranty claims beyond the return window, please refer to the manufacturer's warranty information included with your smartband.
