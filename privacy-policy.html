Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 02/03/2024
This Privacy Policy describes how Synapse Fit ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your purchase and use of our smartbands and related services (collectively, the "Services").

Information We Collect
We collect several types of information in order to provide the Services and improve your experience:
Personal Information: When you purchase a smartband from us, we may collect information such as your name, email address, shipping address, and billing information. Device Information: We collect information about your smartband, such as the device ID, model number, and software version.

Use of Information
We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
To fulfill your orders and provide the Services.
To send you important information about your smartband, including updates, warranty information, and safety notices.
To personalize your experience and provide you with relevant content and offers.
To improve our Services and develop new products and features.
To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Sharing of Information
We will not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except in the following limited circumstances:
With service providers who help us operate our business and provide the Services, such as data storage providers and customer support agents. These service providers are obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.
To comply with legal or regulatory requirements, such as a court order or subpoena. In the event of a business transfer, such as a merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy.

Data Security
We take reasonable steps to protect the information you provide us from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet or electronic storage system is 100% secure.

Your Choices
You have choices about the information you provide to us and how we use it. You can:
Access and update your personal information through your account settings.
Withdraw your consent for the collection of health and fitness data at any time through the smartband app.
Opt out of receiving marketing communications from us.

Children's Privacy
Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. We will take steps to remove the information from our servers.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
